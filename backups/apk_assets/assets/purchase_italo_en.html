General

The purchase process of an Italo ticket takes place on the official website m.italotreno.it.
For any question about the purchase process, please contact Italo's support directly.

Account

To link the ticket to your account, you can insert your access credentials here, or you can save your account information on the app settings to speed up future purchases.
However, you can purchase an Italo ticket without logging in.

Payment

Payment is made directly on Italo's official website, so the app is not an intermediary.
The payment method (Credit Card) can be saved on the app settings or on italotreno.it to speed up the process.
