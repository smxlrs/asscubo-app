Departures and Arrivals

In the lists there is the following information:
  • Station of departure (for departing trains) or station of arrival (for arriving trains)
  • Platform, if available, in the gray box; the number is in blue if it is differente from the scheduled platform
  • Delay of the train, if available. It should be noted that the website viaggiatreno.it roundes at zero all delays less or equal to 5 minutes.

Starred

The station can be added to the Starred group with the dedicated option in the menu.

Create Alarm

To create a new alarm, tap on the menu icon on the right of the train and choose the Alarm option.
