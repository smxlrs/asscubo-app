General

The purchase process of a Trenitalia ticket takes place on the official website www.trenitalia.com.
For any question about the purchase or information about fares and promotions, please go to trenitalia.com or contact Trenitalia's support.

Account

To make a purchase, a Trenitalia account is needed. If you don't have an account, create a new one on trenitalia.com.
You can insert your access credentials here, or you can save your account information on the app settings to speed up future purchases.

Payment

Payment is made directly on Trenitalia's official website, so the app is not an intermediary.
The payment method (Credit Card or PayPal) can be saved on the app settings to speed up the process.

Ticket

The ticket is delivered to the email address specified and it is available on your personal area on trenitalia.com.
In the PRO version, you can also see your tickets on the dedicated section.
The ticket, both for national and local trains, can be shown to the inspector on the screen of the device.

In case of issues

If for technical issues you don't reach the confirmation page, the ticket will not be created and any pending payment will be cancelled automatically by Trenitalia.
Do not board the train before being in possess of the ticket's PDF.
