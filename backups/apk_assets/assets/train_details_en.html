Route

If the train is on transit, the realtime route is shown (if available), otherwise the scheduled timings are displayed.
For each station on the realtime route, there are the following fields:
  • Platform: if available, the platform is show on the right in the gray box; the platform number is colored in blue if it is different from the scheduled platform.
  • Time of arrival: scheduled time of arrival (in black) and the actual/expected time (in red).
  • Time of departure: scheduled time of departure (in black) and the actual time (in red).
  • Stop symbol: the stop symbol, on the left, can have different colors.
    - in blue a passed stop.
    - in red a cancelled stop.
    - in yellow a special stop.
    - gray if none of the previous apply.

Delays

Delays are provided by train companies through official websites (viaggiatreno.it, italotreno.it), so the app is not responsbile for any error or data not updated.
Generally, delays are updated when the train arrives to or departs from a station, or any other checkpoint, so if the train is not running, the delay is not updated.
If you have more information about the train, please share it with other users on the Comments section.

Guaranteed trains on strikes

The trains which are know to be runnning on strikes are marked with a green shield icon.
It is highly recommended to tap on the shield icon to see more details, if available.

Comments

Any user can share in Comments additional information about the train, such as delay, malfunction, location, etc.
Authentication is only used to identify each user; comments are only visible inside the app.
Users are asked to be polite and only post useful information.
The developer retain the right to moderate comments that are offensive or way out of topic.

Pinned Notification

To dismiss the notification, press the 'Dismiss' action in the notification itself. If the action is not visible, expand the notification swiping it down

Share Train

From the main menu, you can share the train status via email, social, text, and so on.

Share Stop

Press the menu icon on the right of the station, then choose the Share option.

Starred

The train can be addedd to the starred group with the Starred option on the main menu.

WidgetPRO

The train can be transferred to a Train widget.
To use this functionality, the app Train Timetable Italy Widget must be install, and at least one Train widget must be present in the Home.

Create AlarmPRO

Tap on the menu icon on the right of the station and choose the Alarm option.
