Recent searches

The recent searches list has the latest 5 searches. To delete a recent search, long-press on it, then press on the trash button.

Starred searchesPRO

In addition to recent searches, you can also have starred searches. To change a recent search to a starred search, long-press on it then tap the star button.
You can also save a starred search from the menu of the search results screen.
To delete a starred search, long-press on it, then press on the trash button.
