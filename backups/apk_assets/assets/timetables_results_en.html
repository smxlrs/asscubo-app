Search Engine

On each solution, it is specified the search engine that provided it (orariotreni, trenitalia.com, trenord.it, italotreno.it).
For any problem concerning the orariotreni search engine, please contact me via email.

Delay

For each train in transit it is shown, on the right, the delay in minutes.
In case of error an esclamation point is displayed.
A train highlighted in yellow indicates that there are variations in the route; look at the details for more information.
The delay is provided by the official websites, so there's no guarantee on their correctness.

Platforms

If the information is available, on the right of departure and arrival stations, the platform is written in blue.
The availability of the platform number depends on the official websites.

Guaranteed trains on strikes

The trains which are know to be runnning on strikes are marked with a green shield icon.
It is highly recommended to open the train details and tap on the shield icon to see more details, if available.

Purchase

With the purchase button (cart) it is possibile to purchase the ticket on the official website, with a simplified procedure.
In case of a rountrip journey, you have to choose the outward solution first (button with the arrow), then the return solutions will be shown.

Prices

For each solution, it is shown the lowest price, if available.
Tapping on the price, it is possible to see more fares.

Save Timetable

With the dedicated button on the actionbar, the timetable can be saved and it will be available on the Saved Timetables section of the app.

Starred

The search (departure and return stations) can be added to Starred searches to be always available.

Share TimetablePRO

You can share the entire timetable with the Share option on the menu, or you can share a single solution .

Add train to calendarPRO

Press on the menu button on the upper right corner of a train to add a train to the calendar.
This functionality is available on Android 4+

Create an alarmPRO

Press on the menu button on the upper right corner of a train to create an alarm on the scheduled departure or arrival time.
