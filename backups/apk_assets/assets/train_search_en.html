Recent trains

The five most recent train are listed in the Recent section.
A larger number of saved trains can be achieved, on the PRO version, with Starred trains.

Delete a train

To delete a train, long press on it and tap on the Delete button.

Starred

To add or remove a train to the Starred list, press on the star button.
A train can also be added to the Starred list from the menu of the details screen.

Custom titlePRO

You can set a custom title to a starred train by long pressing on it and selecting the Edit title option.
