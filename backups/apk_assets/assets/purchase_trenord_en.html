General

The purchase process of a Trenord ticket takes place on the official website www.trenord.it.
For any information about fares and promotions, please refer to the website.

Ticket

The Trenord electronic ticket is delivered to the email address or mobile phone number specified.

Account

The ticket is not linked to your Trenord personal account, however in the app settings you can create a Trenord account with your personal information to speed up the purchase process.

Payment

Payment is made directly on Trenord's official website, so the app is not an intermediary.
The payment method (Credit Card) can be saved on the app settings to speed up the process.
